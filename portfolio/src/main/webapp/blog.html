"How Grace Hopper Impacted Me"

By: Oyin Sholeye

Posted on May 28, 2020

Today, I would like to share how attending Grace Hopper was a very influencial experience for me. Grace Hopper is a conference for women in computing. Before I got to college, I had no idea what Grace Hopper was. One day, as I was sitting in my "Introduction to Computering Engineering" class trying to figure out an assignment, I was approached by one of the TA's. She told me that she noticed that I was in every computer science class and asked if I had considered attending the Grace Hopper Conference. She went on to tell me how she got her internship offer to work at Facebook by going to the conference.I knew that I wanted to intern at a top tech company as well. I decided to apply for the GHC scholarship that my engineering school offered. I didn't expect to be selected for the scholarship, but I am so grateful that I was. My school covered the hotel, flights, and ticket cost for me. Th escholarship didn't cover food, but I was able to get fed through company networking events. I was super excited to go to my first conference. Before arriving in Orlando for the conference, I expected to just go to the career fair and mind my business. I am so happy to say that I made so many new friends.

Day One of Grace Hopper was very overwhelming for me. I was in a space with 27,000 other girls. It also gave me a sense of belonging because these were girls who shared the same passion for technolgy and coding as me. I ended up meeting with a friend from high school while at the conference. It was really nice becuase we got to do a lot of catching up. She even snuck me in an invite-only event in Disney Springs! Day Two of Grace Hopper was so crazy becuase I had never been to a career fair so huge. There were top companies and startups that were looking to recruit. Luckily for me, my nerves were calmed because I prepared ahead of time.

The main reasons why Grace Hopper was so influencial:

1. I was able to interview with Google at the conference which led to me getting and accepting and internship offer for STEP.

2. I was able to build new friendships with some wonderful girls.

Would I do it again? Yes, yes I would!#GHC2020
